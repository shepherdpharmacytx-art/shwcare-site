How It Works
Simple steps. Clear expectations.

We optimize for a frictionless consumer experience while keeping prescription workflows compliant and handled through appropriate licensed entities.

1) Choose a program

Select the lane that matches your goals (weight, recovery, longevity).

2) Start intake

We collect basic contact and routing info (no PHI on this site).

3) Prescription submitted

Preferred: eRx from your provider. Alternate secure channels as instructed.

4) Verification & fulfillment

Appropriately licensed pharmacy partner verifies and dispenses pursuant to a valid prescription.

5) Shipping & tracking

Cold-chain overnight shipping when needed; tracking sent by email.

Need help?

Contact support and we’ll coordinate next steps with your provider/pharmacy partner.
