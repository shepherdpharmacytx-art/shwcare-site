How it works

A consumer-simple flow that stays aligned with pharmacy and provider requirements.

1) Start intake

Choose your goal and submit a short intake request. Avoid entering sensitive medical details on this public site.

2) Provider review

A licensed practitioner evaluates appropriateness. If a prescription is required, it is issued through standard workflows (eRx/fax/portal).

3) Pharmacy routing

Dispensing is routed to the appropriate licensed pharmacy/partner for the patient’s state and therapy requirements.

4) Fill, QA, ship

Order is prepared, quality checked, and shipped according to product handling (ambient vs cold-chain). Tracking is provided.

Note: The public SHW site is education and intake routing. If PHI must be collected, it should be collected inside your pharmacy system (e.g., SRS) or a secured portal designed for PHI.
