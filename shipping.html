Shipping Policy

Shipping timelines and handling procedures vary based on temperature sensitivity and fulfillment requirements.

Temperature-Sensitive Shipments (Cold Chain)

  • Orders may take up to 72 hours to process before leaving the facility.
  • Cold-chain shipments are typically sent overnight with insulated packaging and cold packs.
  • We ship cold-chain items Monday through Thursday to avoid weekend transit delays.
  • Orders placed after 12:00 PM Thursday may ship the following Monday or next business day.
  • Refrigerate immediately upon delivery (if applicable) to maintain product integrity.

Non-Temperature-Sensitive Shipments

  • Most orders ship within 1–2 business days after processing.
  • Orders ship Monday through Friday via USPS/UPS/FedEx depending on destination.
  • Tracking details are sent by email once the shipment leaves the facility.

General Notes

  • Carrier delays are outside our control once handed to the carrier.
  • Please verify your shipping address before submitting an order.
  • During hot weather, retrieve deliveries promptly—especially temperature-sensitive shipments.

Questions? Email support@shwcare.com.
